Our Story

Founded in 2020, BikeDekho started as a small garage project with a big dream — to make premium motorcycles accessible to everyone. What began with just a handful of bikes has grown into one of the most trusted bike marketplaces in the country.

Over the years, we have partnered with the world's leading bike manufacturers to bring you an unparalleled selection of motorcycles, scooters, and accessories. Our team of passionate riders and automotive experts works tirelessly to ensure every product meets the highest standards of quality and performance.

Today, with over 5,000 bikes sold and 12,000 happy customers, we continue to innovate and expand — driven by our love for two wheels and the freedom they represent.

Mission & Vision

Driven by purpose, guided by values — here is what we stand for.

Our Mission

To empower every rider with top-quality motorcycles and gear while fostering a global community of biking enthusiasts who share a passion for adventure and the open road. We strive to make every purchase seamless, every ride safe, and every customer satisfied.

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted motorcycle marketplace — where every ride begins with confidence and every customer feels like family. We envision a future where BikeDekho is synonymous with quality, reliability, and the sheer joy of riding.
